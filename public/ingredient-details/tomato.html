Tomato

Preparation

  • Use hand slicer to create uniform slices
  • Clean slicer before and after each use
  • Pat dry with paper towel before adding to sandwich
  • For prep station: pre-slice only what will be used in 4 hours

Use By

  • Whole tomato: 5-7 days at room temperature
  • Sliced tomato: Use within 4 hours or discard
  • Refrigeration: Not recommended unless sliced (38-40Â°F)
  • Signs of spoilage: Soft spots, mold, excessive juice, fermented smell
