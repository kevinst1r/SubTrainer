Lettuce

Preparation

  • Thoroughly wash leaves under cold water
  • Dry completely using paper towels (pat dry, don't rub)
  • Tear into appropriate sizes (don't cut with knife)
  • Store unused portions in sealed container with paper towel

Use By

  • Once prepped: 2-3 days maximum
  • Refrigeration: Required at all times (34-38Â°F)
  • Signs of spoilage: Browning edges, slimy texture, unpleasant smell
  • Quality check: Inspect during each shift change
